About Our Software

Welcome to my attention evaluation page!
This project is an exciting collaboration of cutting-edge technologies, including Mediapipe, OpenCV, Tkinter, and several others.
Our attention evaluation system is designed to track head and eye movements in real-time to determine levels of engagement and focus.
With this information, we can adapt teaching styles, materials, or alert drivers if they are showing signs of distraction or drowsiness.
This innovative system has broad applications in various fields, including education, transportation, and healthcare.
We are proud to be at the forefront of this technology and invite you to explore our page and learn more about our attention evaluation system.
